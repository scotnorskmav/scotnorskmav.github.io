Useful Phrases in Scot Norse

Background:

Scot Norse is a North Germanic language spoken in Northern Scotland around the area of Wick, where the Norse had once settled. It has various similarities to Faroese as well as very few similarities to Norn (as well as its own). Here are a few useful phrases in Scot Norse (or sometimes referred to as Malavar/Málavár):
